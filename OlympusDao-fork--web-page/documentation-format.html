AtlantisDAO

The Reserve Currency for Terra

What is Atlantis?

Atlantis is the first decantralized reserve currency protocil available on the Terra Network based on the PILLAR token. Each PILLAR token is backed by a basket of assets (e.g. TerraUST, LUNA, TerraKRW) in the Atlantis treasury, giving it an intrinsic value it cannot fall below.

What is the point of Atlantis?

Our goal is to build a policy-controlled currency system, native to the Terra network. WE believe this system can be used to optimize stability so that PILLAR can function as a global unit-of-account and medium-of-exchange currency. The goal is to achieve price flatness for a representative basket of goods without the use of fiat Currency and in doing so promote the freedon the crptocurrency industry needs to free itself from the traditional financial system.

How do I participate in Atlantis?

There are two main strategies for market participants: staking and bonding. Stakes stake their PILLAR token in return for more PILLAR tokens, while minters provide LP tokens through the bonding mechanism in exchange for discounted PILLAR tokens.

Who created Atlantis?

Atlantis, like Wonderland on Avalanche network, Spartacus on Phanton, etc., is a fork of OlympusDAO. Only that our software is not just a deployment but a whole rewrite which allowed us to make several improvements based not only on the experience provided by the OlympusDAO performance but on the Terra specific features, such as speed, scalacility, gas fees, etc.
